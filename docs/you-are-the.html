This piece was made for “100 people’s exhibition,” an exhibition that my colleagues made and joined.

The woman in the picture represents my mother, who likes drawing and making things.

I made this piece to encourage her and to thank her for sharing her interesting ideas and stories with family.

You are the origin of my inspirations

#8

Description

The words in the background say “You are the origin of my inspirations,” which is the title of the piece. The shape and color of the character are parody of The Statue of Liberty. .    It was made as digital art and I printed on a canvas, so that the format goes well with the content that’s emphasizing artistic feature of character.

 

Printed on canvas

Reflection

I consider this one as one of my most “artistic” work, since it tells my own story to people. And I found myself trying more to develop the message rather than making it effective or beautiful. It was a little refreshing for me to do something like this.
