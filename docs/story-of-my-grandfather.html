This small book I made is about my grandfather’s life and stories.

My father used to record grandfather’s words sometimes.

I used the recorded files and additional conversation with my grandfather as contents for the book.

I edited in the way of doing visualization of the conversation between my grandfather and family members.

There are topics, such as money, proverbs, younger years, and so on.

Story of My Grandfather

Description

As this takes recorded files as source, I wrote the day the conversation was recorded and topic on the top left corners.

I tried to convey the feeling of the sound file by adjusting the spaces between the words, letters or lines according to the speed of grandfather’s actual speaking.

Finally,  put other family member’s words in smaller font size and tried to make words float around grandfather’s words just like in the recorded audio.
