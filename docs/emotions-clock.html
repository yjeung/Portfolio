This mobile application is for recording emotions felt in specific moments of a day.

I used a flower garden as the theme of this project, so emotions are shown as animated plants.

I wished that it could work as a kind of emotional therapy, making users feel better by knowing admitting their emotion now and expressing it in a playable way.

I made it in a “media design project” class with adobe flash and exported to format for Android smart phones.

Emotions Clock

Description

Users can tap on the current time and choose between 4 emotions represented by rocks with facial emotions.

Different animated plants are made for choosing different emotions.

The plants can be moved to the desired place and have tag that describes the time it created.

 

I made the background change accordingly to the current time period to make it feel more like it’s related to the real world rather than just a digital space containing emotion state information.
