Meat Free Monday
Identity Redesign

Identity design

2017 winter Individual

Meat-Free Monday is a campaign that makes people think again about the impact of eating meat. While re-designing, I focused on how to make the brand more attractive, while keeping the current brand image by making it feel warm, natural, and free. For specific graphic style, I kept the image of Heart with face and the slab-serif font. Most of the recipes are from its original website.

Process

To specify the brand identity, there many modifications of symbol were made. Finally, current form of red outlined heart with green M-shaped legs with its eyes closed was chosen, as it conveys the meaning of love, free and nature friendly feeling effectively. Starting with this, Brand Menual Book was made to give the brand a consistent look and feel. identity key concept: Joyful, Vivid, Characterized. Brand Manual Book

Advertsement Means - Album Series "Meat Free Monday With Music"

One of the special story of Meat Free Monday Campaign is that it was launched by Paul Mccartney, the Beatles' former member, and Many other celebrities including singers are supporting the campaign. It actually makes people interested in the campaign and be familiar. Therefore, adding on Brand Menual Book, a concept compilation album series was made to privide the campaign a channel to communicate with people.

The album cover is just a single craft A4-sized paper. Its first page has illustration of animals we protect in this campaign, explanation of why this campaign is making this album series, and one of the goals that can be achieved by joying the campaign. On the back, it has QR code that links to the site, and recipes from the site.

This design of front/back cover makes the album cover work as a poster or as recipe, not only as a cover. The series are made of 3 versions, cheerful, fun and calm songs. Songs are carefully chosen with this concept, that consist of Paul Mccartney and MFM supporters'. In addition, celebrating that this Christmas(2017) is on Monday, Christmas version was made.
